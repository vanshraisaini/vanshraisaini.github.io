Who I Am

I am currently pursuing my Master’s in Robotics at the UC San Diego’s Jacobs School of Engineering. My interests span perception, SLAM, reinforcement learning, and control, with an emphasis on developing reliable systems that work in real-world environments.

Before joining UC San Diego, I worked as a Robotics Software Engineer at Unbox Robotics, where I developed and deployed algorithms for autonomous mobile robots. My work involved solving practical challenges in multi robot navigation, system reliability, and real-world deployment—experiences that shaped my approach to building robust robotic systems.

I completed my undergraduate studies at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Jammu, where I was actively involved in robotics and hands-on system development. Through projects and collaborations, I developed a strong foundation in integrating perception, planning, and control into complete robotic systems.
